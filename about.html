Our Objective

Our goal for TherapAI is to provide a safe space online for war veterans with PTSD to seek support. Frequently, people with PTSD tend to withdraw from friends and family. TherapAI aims to avoid common concerns associated with visiting a human therapist, such as confidentiality, judgement, and stress.

The Technicals

TherapAI will be trained on a wide variety of data, consisting of videos and pictures of different emotions. The AI will pick up on slight differences in tone, facial cues, keywords, and body language to accurately access the patients current emotional state. What counts as success for our AI? Our goal is for TherapAI to seem as human-like as possible, be able to accurately summarize sessions, and give diagonoses or suggestions supported by evidence.
