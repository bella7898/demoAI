Societal Impacts

TherapAI may develop bias during training or focus on arbitrary values when trying to understand human emotion. The AI may develop values that do not align with the client's. Additionally, using AI as a means of therapy may lead to destimatization of mental health, discomfort and apprehension, lack of emotional connection, and a possiblity of data leaks. One question we need to ask is how effective and reliable are diagnosis made by an AI? On the other hand, development of technology similar to TherapAI may have many positive impacts on society. Therapy will become more accessible and cheaper, it may enhance productivity, help train new psychologists, and improve studies on human behavior.
